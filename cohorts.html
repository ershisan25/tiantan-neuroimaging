Cohorts & Registries

Multi-centre study infrastructure

Prospective and retrospective imaging cohorts registered on ClinicalTrials.gov, underpinning the group's biomarker discovery and validation.

NORMAL · NCT06643858

Lifespan brain-imaging norms

The NORMAL Chinese lifespan brain-charts initiative — the group reports 20,000+ datasets from 20+ centres to establish developmental and ageing brain-imaging norms for the Chinese population. Related lifespan charts were published in Nature Neuroscience 2025 (C-LBM consortium, with Beijing Normal University).

CLUE · NCT04106830

Neuroinflammation imaging cohort

CLinical and imaging patterns of neUroinflammation disEases — 2,000+ clinical-imaging-genomic datasets (MS, NMOSD) to develop brain and spinal-cord biomarkers; uses DIR, DKI, QSM and resting-state fMRI.

HOPE · NCT05859659

Neuro-oncology imaging database

Clinical, patHOlogical and imaging Project of nEuro-oncology — the group reports 30,000+ brain-tumour clinical-imaging-pathology cases from 20 centres (2021 WHO CNS5), for deep-learning models of molecular pathology and prognosis.

MAGNET · NCT05754476

Contrast-agent reduction

Multicentre study of an AI model that synthesises virtual contrast-enhanced brain MRI to reduce gadolinium-based contrast-agent dose.
