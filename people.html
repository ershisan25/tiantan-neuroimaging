Principal Investigator

Prof. Yaou Liu 刘亚欧, M.D., Ph.D.

Director of the Department of Radiology at Beijing Tiantan Hospital, Capital Medical University (China National Clinical Research Center for Neurological Diseases). He holds two doctoral degrees — from Capital Medical University (2013) and VU University Amsterdam (2017) — and trained at the Neuroscience Center of the University of Melbourne, the University of Antwerp, and VU University Medical Center. His research develops and validates imaging markers for major neurological diseases, especially multiple sclerosis and neuro-oncology. He has published 110+ papers — including in Lancet Neurology, Nature Protocols, Neurology, Radiology and Medical Image Analysis — and holds 6 licensed patents.

Vice-director · CSR Youth Group Committee member · PACTRIMS Young “Beijing Scholar” “Ten-Thousand-Talents” Young Leading Talent Google Scholar ↗

Selected honors: Du Pré Award & McDonald Fellowship (International MS Federation) · Neuroradiology Scholarship (European Society of Radiology) · 20+ neuroimaging awards.
