The group & its members

Department of Radiology, Beijing Tiantan Hospital — radiologists, scientists, clinicians and trainees working on imaging biomarkers for neurological disease, with international collaborators.

Group Leader

Prof. Yaou Liu 刘亚欧, M.D., Ph.D.

Director of the Department of Radiology at Beijing Tiantan Hospital, Capital Medical University (China National Clinical Research Center for Neurological Diseases). Two doctoral degrees — Capital Medical University (2013) and VU University Amsterdam (2017); trained at the University of Melbourne, the University of Antwerp and VU University Medical Center. 110+ papers (incl. Lancet Neurology, Nature Protocols), 6 patents.

Vice-director · CSR Youth GroupCommittee · PACTRIMS Young “Beijing Scholar” Google Scholar ↗

Honors: Du Pré Award & McDonald Fellowship (MSIF) · ESR Neuroradiology Scholarship · 20+ neuroimaging awards.
